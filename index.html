Elapsed Roleplay

Official RP Rulebook

(2025 Final Edition)

Welcome to Elapsed Roleplay

A server built for immersive, realistic, and fair roleplay. This rulebook defines how players interact, ensuring everyone has a fun, structured, and engaging experience.

⚠️ MANDATORY READING

This guide is mandatory reading for all players. Ignorance of the rules does not exempt you from consequences.

1

1. General Rules

1.1 Respect & Conduct

• Treat all players with respect - no harassment, bullying, or discrimination

• Keep In-Character (IC) and Out-of-Character (OOC) separate

• No spam in chat, voice, or commands

✅ Good RP Example

You are robbed by another player. You comply IC and plan retaliation later through proper RP.

❌ Fail RP Example

You start insulting the robbers in OOC chat.

1.2 Cheating & Exploits

• Any use of cheats, hacks, macros, or third-party tools is strictly prohibited

• Exploiting bugs for gain results in bans and account wipes

2

2. Core RP Principles

2.1 Value of Life (VoL)

Every life matters. Do not act recklessly when threatened or outnumbered.

✅ Good RP

You comply when three armed robbers hold you at gunpoint.

❌ Fail RP

You pull a gun on five armed cops during a traffic stop.

2.2 Metagaming

Do not use OOC information for IC advantage.

2.3 Powergaming

Do not force actions on other players without giving them a chance to respond.

2.4 New Life Rule (NLR)

• After death, forget all events leading to that death

• Wait 15 minutes before returning to location

3

3. Robbery Rules

3.1 Cooldowns

Minor Robberies (House, Store, ATM, Fleeca): 15 minutes

Major Robberies (Jewelry, Paleto, Pacific): 30 minutes

3.2 Crew Limits

• Most robberies: 3 robbers + 2 backup

• House robbery: 2 robbers + 1 backup

• Pacific Bank: 4 robbers + 2 backup

• Police limit: Maximum 8 officers

Robbery Types

🏠 House Robbery

Crew: 2 + 1 backup | Hostages: Not allowed

Scenario: Quiet break-in, steal valuables, leave before police

🏪 Store/ATM Robbery

Crew: 3 + 2 backup | Hostages: 1 | Police: Min 2

Scenario: Hold cashier/civilian hostage, negotiate with police

4

Bank Robberies

🏛️ Fleeca Bank

Crew: 3 + 2 backup | Hostages: 1

Police: Min 3 / Max 8

💎 Jewelry Store

Crew: 3 + 2 backup | Hostages: 1

Police: Min 4

🏦 Paleto Bank

Crew: 3 + 2 backup | Hostages: 1

Police: Min 4

🏛️ Pacific Bank

Crew: 4 + 2 backup | Hostages: 2

Police: Max 8

4. Hostage Rules

• Must be real players

• Can only be harmed if police refuse negotiation

• Must be treated with realistic RP

5

Police & Gang Rules

👮 Police Behavior

• Act professional, negotiate first

• Cannot camp or abuse OOC knowledge

• Announce charges and read rights

⚡ Gang Rules

• Max 5 active members per operation

• Turf wars require staff notification

• Code raids: Police max 8, Gang defenders max 5

Punishments

1st: Warning → 2nd: Fine/Jail → 3rd: Temp Ban → Severe: Perm Ban
