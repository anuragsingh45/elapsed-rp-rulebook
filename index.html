Elapsed Roleplay – Official RP Rulebook
(2025 Final Edition)
Introduction

Welcome to Elapsed Roleplay, a server built for immersive, realistic, and fair roleplay. This rulebook defines how players — including civilians, criminals, gangs, and law enforcement — interact, ensuring everyone has a fun, structured, and engaging experience.

This guide is mandatory reading for all players. Ignorance of the rules does not exempt you from consequences.

1.1 Respect & Conduct

Treat all players with respect. No harassment, bullying, or discrimination. Keep IC and OOC separate.

Good RP: You are robbed by another player. You comply IC and plan retaliation later.
Fail RP: You start insulting the robbers in OOC chat.

Do not spam chat, voice, or text commands to disrupt RP.

1.2 Cheating & Exploits

No cheats, hacks, macros, or third-party tools. Exploiting bugs = bans and wipes.

Good RP: You discover a bug that duplicates money and report it.
Fail RP: You exploit a bug to duplicate money repeatedly.
