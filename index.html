Elapsed Roleplay – Official RP Rulebook
(2025 Final Edition) — Introduction
Welcome

Welcome to Elapsed Roleplay, a server built for immersive, realistic, and fair roleplay. This rulebook defines how players — including civilians, criminals, gangs, and law enforcement — interact, ensuring everyone has a fun, structured, and engaging experience. It also provides clear examples and explanations for each rule.

Mandatory

This guide is mandatory reading for all players. Ignorance of the rules does not exempt you from consequences.

1. General Rules
1.1 Respect & Conduct
Respect

Treat all players with respect at all times. No harassment, bullying, or discrimination.

Keep In-Character (IC) and Out-of-Character (OOC) separate.

Good RP

You are robbed by another player. You comply IC and plan retaliation later through proper RP.

Fail RP

You start insulting the robbers in OOC chat.

Chat Conduct

Do not spam chat, voice, or text commands to disrupt RP.

1. General Rules
1.2 Cheating & Exploits / 1.3 Admin & Staff
Cheating & Exploits

Any use of cheats, hacks, macros, or third-party tools is strictly prohibited.

Exploiting bugs for gain (money, weapons, items) will result in bans and wipe of affected accounts.

Good RP Example

You discover a bug that duplicates money and report it to admins.

Fail RP Example

You exploit a bug to duplicate money multiple times.

Admin Compliance

Follow instructions from staff immediately during RP. Disputes or appeals must go through official ticket systems; do not argue IC or OOC during active situations.

2. Core RP Principles
2.1 Value of Life (VoL) & 2.2 Metagaming
Value of Life (VoL)

Every life matters. Do not act recklessly when threatened or outnumbered.

Good RP

You comply when three armed robbers hold you at gunpoint.

Fail RP

You pull a gun on five armed cops during a traffic stop.

Metagaming

Do not use OOC information for IC advantage.

Fail RP Example: Watching a live stream to track a player’s location in IC.

2. Core RP Principles
2.3 Powergaming & 2.4 NLR
Powergaming

Do not force actions or outcomes on other players without giving them a chance to respond.

Fail RP

“I instantly knock you out” without allowing reaction.

New Life Rule (NLR)

After death, forget all events leading to that death. Wait 15 minutes before returning to that location.

Fail RP

Returning immediately to exact revenge after respawn.

Practical

Treat NLR seriously in all RP interactions — resuming prior grudges or targeting the same location breaks immersion and can be penalized.

3. Robbery Rules
3.1 Cooldowns & 3.2 Crew Limits
Cooldowns

Minor Robberies (House, Store, ATM, Fleeca Bank): 15 minutes

Major Robberies (Jewelry, Paleto, Pacific Bank): 30 minutes

Crew & Limits

Most robberies: 3 robbers + 2 backup. House robbery: 2 robbers + 1 backup. Pacific Bank: 4 robbers + 2 backup (6 total).

Police Limit

Police limit: Maximum 8 officers per robbery (unless specifically stated otherwise for big events).

Reasoning

These limits exist to keep heists tactical and fair — not a numbers stomp. Abusing crew limits will be acted on by staff.

3.3 Robbery Types
House / Store / ATM
House Robbery

Crew: 2 + 1 backup • Hostages: Not allowed • Cooldown: 15 min

Scenario: Two robbers quietly break into a house. One backup waits in a vehicle outside. They steal valuables and leave before police arrive.

Good RP

Breaking in stealthily, interacting with NPCs realistically, leaving calmly.

Store Robbery

Crew: 3 + 2 backup • Hostages: 1 • Police: Min 2

Scenario: Robbers hold cashier hostage, demand money. Backup waits in a car outside.

Fail RP

Shoot hostage randomly or ignore police arrival.

ATM Robbery

Crew: 3 + 2 backup • Hostages: 1 • Police: Min 2

Scenario: Robbers drill ATM, backup scouts for police. Upon police arrival, negotiation occurs.

3.3 Robbery Types
Fleeca & Jewelry
Fleeca Bank

Crew: 3 + 2 backup • Hostages: 1 • Police: Min 3 / Max 8

Scenario: Three robbers breach vault; backup waits outside for getaway. Police negotiate.

Good RP

Follow IC negotiation, release hostage safely.

Jewelry Store

Crew: 3 + 2 backup • Hostages: 1 • Police: Min 4

Scenario: Smash display cases, hold one hostage, interact with arriving police for negotiation.

Fail RP

Using more than allowed crew or ignoring negotiation.

3.3 Robbery Types
Paleto & Pacific Bank
Paleto Bank

Crew: 3 + 2 backup • Hostages: 1 • Police: Min 4

Scenario: Robbery with coordinated backup, realistic escape, hostage management.

Pacific Bank

Crew: 4 + 2 backup • Hostages: 2 • Police: Max 8

Scenario: Larger heist with multi-hostage management, timed escape, police coordination.

4. Hostage Rules
Handling & Limits
Core Rules

Must be real players. Can only be harmed if police refuse negotiation or hostage attacks.

Fail RP

Killing hostages after demands are met.

Realism

Hostages must be treated with realistic RP and not be used as props. Staff reserves judgment on ambiguous situations.

5. Backup Rules
Support & Timing
Entry Rules

Backup cannot start action before main crew initiates. Must remain outside until called in.

Coordination

Communicate roles (driver, lookout, negotiator). Uncoordinated backups who break immersion may be penalized.

Consequences

If backup acts as main actor early or undermines RP, staff can warn, fine, or restrict backup from future operations.

6. Code Raid Rules
Authorization & Limits
Limits

Police max: 8. Gang defenders max: 5 (unless admin approved).

Cooldown: 1 hour per location.

Authorization

Must be authorized by commanding officer. Unauthorized raids may be canceled or punished.

Scenario

Police raid gang stash house, use tactical approach, negotiation, hostages if required.

7. Police Behaviour Rules
Conduct & Examples
Core Conduct

Act professional, negotiate first, avoid unnecessary aggression. Cannot camp or abuse OOC knowledge. Announce charges and read rights.

Good RP

Blocking roads, negotiation

Fail RP

Shooting hostages immediately

Guidance

Police should use measured tactics and avoid OOC-driven actions; staff will review ambiguous incidents.

8. Gang & Turf Rules
Membership & Wars
Members

Max 5 active members per operation.

Turf wars require escalation and staff notification.

Turf War Conduct

Organize with your gang leadership and notify staff for large turf events for fairness and oversight.

Penalties

Abuse of turf rules, griefing, or using unauthorized numbers may be punished (warning → fine/jail → temp/permanent ban).

9. Vehicle & Safe Zone Rules
Driving & Zones
Vehicle Conduct

No VDM. Drive realistically. Treat vehicles as part of RP and avoid reckless driving that disrupts immersion.

Safe Zones

Hospitals, PD, Courts are safe zones — combat and aggressive RP are not allowed inside these areas unless scripted by staff.

Use Cases

If you need to move a roleplay event near a safe zone, coordinate with staff to avoid rule conflicts.

10. Economy & Loot Guidelines
Fairness & Anti-Abuse
Looting

Loot fairly based on RP scenario. Keep rewards balanced to preserve server economy and fun.

No Duplication

No duplication or exploiting economy. Any discovered exploits must be reported to staff immediately.

Sanctions

Exploitation of the economy will result in item/funds removal and possible bans.

11. OOC Rules & Punishments
Community & Enforcement
OOC Use

Use /ooc for support/questions. Do not use OOC to harass, insult, or spam the community.

Reporting

Report issues via tickets. Staff will review and respond; avoid public arguments that escalate situations.

Punishments

Punishments: Warning → Fine/Jail → Temporary/Permanent Ban

Enforcement

Multiple infractions escalate penalties. Severe or repeated abuse may lead to permanent removal.

12. Example Scenarios & Case Studies
Learning by Example
Robbery Walkthrough

Step-by-step robbery, police response, negotiation, escape. Follow RP cues and coordinate with police if present.

Hostage Management

Hostage management in Fleeca, Pacific Bank — always prioritize realistic negotiation and safety of players used as hostages.

Code Raid Scenario

Gang vs Police Code Raid scenario — plan, notify staff, and keep defender/attacker numbers within limits.
